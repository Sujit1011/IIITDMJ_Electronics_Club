About

The Electronics Club is a hobby group that aims to teach and help people understand the electronic gadgets in the world today, and also assists people in developing their own devices. We conduct various events and sessions throughout the year to bring out the technology in you. The Electronics Club envisions to nurture your engineering maturity by giving you practical and hands on exposure. We aim to fill in the void of Electronics hobbyists in the institute by providing a platform where the theory learnt in classes can be applied in the real world.






Activities



Sessions are arranged whole year to teach the members about the concepts of electronics and circuits as well as different electronic components. Different competitions are organised in the meanwhile such as

Circuit Master

Innovation Contest

LED Matrix Gaming

Matlab Workshop

Embedded System Workshop





Articles & Events

Article 1: Name

The club’s official name will be “ELECTRONICS CLUB”. No other name will be used in the advertisement or representation of the club.

Article 2: Clue Mandidate

The club’s mandate will be as follows:

  • Mission : Electronics Club exists to provide exciting and unique opportunities for group student a practical understanding of electronic gadgets and fun in learning concepts. The Club will strive to bring students together for teamwork, and to unite them under the common objective of having fun in making projects.

  • Strategy : The Club will hold unconventional way of learning that are tailored to innovative students, and which result in unique and memorable experiences.

  • Vision : Community building amongst students and to impart members with learning and fun memories that will last a lifetime.

Article 3: Membership

• The club’s membership is open to all students of institute and one can become the member of the club by filling the membership form and paying registration fee of Rs50.

• All member must be regular to sessions and meetings of the club. Membership of any member will be canceled in case he/she is not active in club activities.

• The membership can also be terminated anytime on the behalf of any misconduct or misbehavior as witnessed during the sessions.

• Only the club members would be allowed to use club’s property and the components would only be issued to club members. Fine will be imposed if anyone will be found damaging club property.

Article 4: Executive Responsibities

• The coordinator will organize club’s sessions, meetings, events and competitions in accordance with its Constitution’s mandate.

• Club coordinator will be responsible for submitting event proposals, club activities report and budget to higher authorities.

• The co-coordinator will be there to assist coordinator in all club activities.
